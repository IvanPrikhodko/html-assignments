"The Country Heaven" is a charming single-family ranch located at 5981 Don Mills Road, Toronto, Ontario. It is priced at $780,000 and sits on a lot size of 0.15 acres. The property features three bedrooms and two bathrooms, including a master suite with a walk-in closet and en-suite bathroom. The first floor offers an open concept living and dining area, a gourmet kitchen with granite countertops and stainless steel appliances, and a half bathroom. The second floor features two additional bedrooms and a full bathroom. The property also includes a two-car garage and a large backyard with a patio and a garden.
