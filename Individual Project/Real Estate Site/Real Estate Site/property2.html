"The City Dweller's Dream" is a modern townhouse located at 937 King Street West, Toronto, Ontario. It is priced at $850,000 and sits on a lot size of 0.05 acres. The property features three bedrooms and three bathrooms, including a master suite with a balcony and en-suite bathroom. The first floor offers an open concept living and dining area, a gourmet kitchen with granite countertops and stainless steel appliances, and a half bathroom. The property also includes a one-car garage and a private backyard.
