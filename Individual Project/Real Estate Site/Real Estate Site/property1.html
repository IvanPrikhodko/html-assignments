"The Victorian" - is a spacious two-story Victorian home located at 22 Elmwood Avenue, Toronto, Ontario. It is priced at $1.2 million and sits on a lot size of 0.25 acres. The property boasts a total of six bedrooms and four bathrooms, including a master suite with a walk-in closet and en-suite bathroom. The first floor features a formal living room with a fireplace, a dining room, a cozy den, a gourmet kitchen with stainless steel appliances, and a half bathroom. The second floor offers a family room, a home office, and four additional bedrooms. The property also features a finished basement with a recreation room, a laundry room, and a storage room.
