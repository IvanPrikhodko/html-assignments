Protected Areas Issues

Created in 1948, Protected Areas Issues is now the worlds largest and most diverse environmental network, harnessing the knowledge, resources and reach of our more than 1,400 Member organisations and 15,000 experts. This diversity and expertise makes Protected Areas Issues the global authority on the status of the natural world and the measures needed to safeguard it.
